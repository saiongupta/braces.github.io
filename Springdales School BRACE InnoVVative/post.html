Earthquake

Measures

After an earthquake, check yourself and others for injuries. Provide first aid for anyone who needs it. Check water, gas, and electric lines for damage. If you smell gas, turn off the main gas valve. Listen to the radio for important updates.

Flood

Measures

After a flood, avoid floodwaters as they may be contaminated. Return home only when authorities say it is safe. Clean and disinfect everything that got wet. Wear protective clothing and be cautious of areas where floodwaters have receded as they may be unstable.

Hurricane

Measures

After a hurricane, stay indoors until it is safe to go outside. Avoid flooded areas and stay away from downed power lines. Inspect your home for damage and photograph the damage for insurance purposes. Make any necessary repairs to prevent further damage.

Wildfire

Measures

After a wildfire, return home only when authorities say it is safe. Be alert for hot spots or smoldering areas. Wear a mask to avoid inhaling dust or ash. Wet debris to minimize dust and be cautious of burned structures as they may be unstable.
